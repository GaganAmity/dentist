Our team of experts


ORAL SURGEON

Dr.Priyesh Panjikar


Dr Priyesh Panjikar has done his Bachelor of dental surgery from Rajiv Gandhi University as well as his Master in dental surgery in the field of Oral and Maxillofacial surgery from the same.

He has done his fellowship in Advanced Maxillofacial trauma from Hosmat multi-speciality Hospital in Bangalore. He has done his fellowship in Maxillofacial Implantology certified from International Council of Oral Implantology (ICOI).
He has done his fellowship in Facial Esthetics from ILAMED. Has served as an observer in Oral Oncology at Kidwai memorial institute of cancer research.


ORAL SURGEON

Dr. Apoorva Panjikar


Dr Apoorva Jha Panjikar has done her bachelor of dental surgery from rajiv gandhi university as well as her master in dental surgery in the field of orthodontist and dentofacial orthopaedics from the same.
She has done her M. Ortho gives her the ability to practice all over the world. Has her diploma in advanced endodontics.
